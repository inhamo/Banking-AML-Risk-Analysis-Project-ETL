### METRICS

| Metric | Formula | What It Measures | Best For |
|--------|----------|-------------------|----------|
| **Customer Base Growth** | `((Current Total Customers - Previous Total Customers) / Previous Total Customers) * 100` | Overall business size expansion | Strategic planning, investor reporting |
| **New Customer MoM Growth** | `((New Customers Current Month - New Customers Previous Month) / New Customers Previous Month) * 100` | Short-term acquisition momentum | Monthly performance, campaign effectiveness |
| **YoY New Customer Growth** | `((New Customers Current Period - New Customers Same Period Last Year) / New Customers Same Period Last Year) * 100` | Seasonal comparison of acquisition | Yearly planning, removing seasonality |
