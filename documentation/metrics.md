### METRICS

## Customer Metrics

| Metric | Formula | What It Measures | Best For |
|--------|----------|-------------------|----------|
| **Customer Base Growth** | $\frac{(\text{Current Total Customers} - \text{Previous Total Customers})}{\text{Previous Total Customers}} \times 100$ | Overall business size expansion | Strategic planning, investor reporting |
| **New Customer MoM Growth** | $\frac{(\text{New Customers Current Month} - \text{New Customers Previous Month})}{\text{New Customers Previous Month}} \times 100$ | Short-term acquisition momentum | Monthly performance, campaign effectiveness |
| **YoY New Customer Growth** | $\frac{(\text{New Customers Current Period} - \text{New Customers Same Period Last Year})}{\text{New Customers Same Period Last Year}} \times 100$ | Seasonal comparison of acquisition | Yearly planning, removing seasonality |
| **Customer Churn Rate** | $\frac{\text{Lost Customers}}{\text{Starting Customers}} \times 100$ | How many customers leave | Retention strategy |
| **Customer Retention Rate** | $\frac{\text{Customers End Period} - \text{New Customers}}{\text{Customers Start Period}} \times 100$ | Bank’s ability to retain customers | Long-term growth |
| **Average Products per Customer** | $\frac{\text{Total Active Products}}{\text{Total Customers}}$ | Relationship depth | Cross-sell analysis |
| **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)** | $\text{Avg Revenue per Customer} \times \text{Avg Customer Tenure}$ | Long-term customer value | Segmentation, prioritisation |

## Product & Portfolio Metrics

| Metric | Formula | What It Measures | Best For |
|--------|----------|------------------|----------|
| **Product Penetration Rate** | $\frac{\text{Customers Using Product}}{\text{Total Customers}} \times 100$ | Product adoption | Product planning |
| **Loan Utilisation Rate** | $\frac{\text{Outstanding Loan Balance}}{\text{Approved Limit}} \times 100$ | Customer usage of credit | Portfolio analysis |
| **Active Accounts Ratio** | $\frac{\text{Active Accounts}}{\text{Total Accounts}} \times 100$ | Real engagement levels | Operational health |


## Revenue Metrics

| Metric | Formula | What It Measures | Best For |
|--------|----------|------------------|----------|
| **Net Interest Margin (NIM)** | $\frac{\text{Interest Income - Interest Expense}}{\text{Earning Assets}} \times 100$ | Lending profitability | CFO, Treasury |
| **Fee Income Growth** | $\frac{\text{Current Fee Income - Previous}}{\text{Previous}} \times 100$ | Growth in non-interest income | Digital, product teams |
| **Customer Profitability** | $\text{Total Revenue} - \text{Total Cost per Customer}$ | Profit generated per customer | Customer segmentation |


## Credit & Risk Metrics

| Metric | Formula | What It Measures | Best For |
|--------|----------|------------------|----------|
| **NPL Ratio (Non-Performing Loans)** | $\frac{\text{NPLs}}{\text{Total Loans}} \times 100$ | Credit portfolio quality | Risk oversight |
| **Credit Approval Rate** | $\frac{\text{Approved Applications}}{\text{Total Applications}} \times 100$ | Underwriting performance | Sales + Credit |
| **Early Delinquency Rate (1–30 Days)** | $\frac{\text{Early Past-Due Amounts}}{\text{Total Loans}} \times 100$ | Early stress signals | Risk monitoring |
| **Charge-Off Rate** | $\frac{\text{Loan Write-offs}}{\text{Average Loan Portfolio}} \times 100$ | Irrecoverable losses | Portfolio risk |


## Operational Metrics

| Metric | Formula | What It Measures | Best For |
|--------|----------|------------------|----------|
| **Account Opening TAT** | $\text{Average Time to Open Account}$ | Efficiency of onboarding | Branch operations |
| **Transaction Success Rate** | $\frac{\text{Successful Transactions}}{\text{Total Transactions}} \times 100$ | System reliability | Digital & IT |
| **Call Resolution Rate** | $\frac{\text{Resolved Cases}}{\text{Total Cases}} \times 100$ | Service quality | Customer support |


## Digital & Engagement Metrics

| Metric | Formula | What It Measures | Best For |
|--------|----------|------------------|----------|
| **Digital Adoption Rate** | $\frac{\text{Digital Users}}{\text{Total Customers}} \times 100$ | Shift to digital channels | Digital strategy |
| **App Engagement Rate** | $\frac{\text{DAU}}{\text{MAU}} \times 100$ | Mobile app stickiness | Product, UX |
| **Failed Login Rate** | $\frac{\text{Failed Logins}}{\text{Total Logins}} \times 100$ | UX friction & security issues | IT & Security |
