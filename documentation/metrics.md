### METRICS

| Metric | Formula | What It Measures | Best For |
|--------|----------|-------------------|----------|
| **Customer Base Growth** | $\frac{(\text{Current Total Customers} - \text{Previous Total Customers})}{\text{Previous Total Customers}} \times 100$ | Overall business size expansion | Strategic planning, investor reporting |
| **New Customer MoM Growth** | $\frac{(\text{New Customers Current Month} - \text{New Customers Previous Month})}{\text{New Customers Previous Month}} \times 100$ | Short-term acquisition momentum | Monthly performance, campaign effectiveness |
| **YoY New Customer Growth** | $\frac{(\text{New Customers Current Period} - \text{New Customers Same Period Last Year})}{\text{New Customers Same Period Last Year}} \times 100$ | Seasonal comparison of acquisition | Yearly planning, removing seasonality |
